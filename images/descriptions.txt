    Price: 1 points
    Description: Activate this card to draw a card from the deck.

3. Heal Card

    Price: 2 points
    Description: Activate this card to gain 2 health.

4. Shield Card

    Price: 3 points
    Description: Activate this card to block the next incoming attack.

5. Poison Card

6. Double Attack Card

    Price: 3 points
    Description: Activate this card to cause 2 damage to the opponent.

7. Energy Boost Card

    Price: 2 points
    Description: Activate this card to gain 3 points immediately.

8. Reflect Card

    Price: 4 points
    Description: Activate this card to reflect the next incoming attack back at the attacker.

9. Sacrifice Card

    Price: 1 point
    Description: Activate this card to lose 3 health but gain 5 points.

10. Steal Card

    Price: 4 points
    Description: Activate this card to steal 2 points from your opponent.
